Data Set Variance

This box plot looks at the variation in the data among the data sets. The percentage of students of color has a larger variation, with a lot of outliers. Percentage of English Language Learners also has a large number of outliers, although the distance between the upper and lower quartile is very small. Percentage of Special Education students and percentage of residents in poverty both have a smaller variation as well. This is evident in the regression plots, as the data points are weighted heavily in the lower part of the x-axis.
