Does population density correlate with the percentage of identified special education students?

R-squared = 0.00331
Correlation = -0.06

The x-axis represents the size of the population within the district, according to the Census Bureau, and the y-axis represents the percent of students receiving special education services. Most districts fall within a very narrow range. A correlation coefficient of -0.06 indicates that there is not much association between these two data sets. It would be interesting to look more closely at the outliers in this dataset to see if anything can be learned about the special education population in those districts.
